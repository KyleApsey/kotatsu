# Cleaning Schedule

## Daily
- Make bed (Morning)
- Wipe bathroom counters (Morning)
- load/unload dishwasher (Morning)
- Clean kitchen counters (Evening)
- Sweep kitchen floor (Evening)
- Put things back in place – 5-min reset (Evening)
- Take out trash, if needed (Evening)

## Weekly

### Monday – Bathroom
- Clean toilet, sink, mirror
- Scrub shower/tub
- Replace towels

### Tuesday – Bedroom
- Change bed sheets
- Dust surfaces
- Vacuum floors
- Declutter nightstands/side of bed

### Wednesday – Kitchen
- Wipe appliances
- Clean sink
- Mop floor

### Thursday – Living Areas
- Dust furniture, TV, shelves
- Vacuum rugs/sweep floors
- Wipe high-touch surfaces (switches, remotes, etc.)

### Friday – Floors focus
- Vacuum/sweep whole house
- Mop all hard wood

### Saturday – Laundry + Reset
- Catch up on laundry
- Organize clutter hotspots (entryway, tables, countertops, etc.)

### Sunday – Light day / Rest
- Only essentials (dishes, quick tidy)
- Aquarium waterchange/maintenance

## Monthly
- Clean inside refrigerator
- Dust vents (ceiling)
- Wash baseboards & doors
- Vacuum under furniture
- Clean windows/mirrors
- Clean inside microwave/oven
- Clean the espresso machine

## Quarterly – Every 3 months or each season
- Rotate mattresses
- Wash curtains
- Deep clean carpets/rugs
- Clean garage/storage areas
- Replace furnace filters
